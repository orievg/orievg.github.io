ABOUT

Asteroid game or "Astgame" is a video game made by the diploma program students from Sweden. Pursuing our interest in Physics, as well as passion for programming we decided to make a small computer game for our CAS (Creativity-Action-Service) project. While working on the project everyone is pursuing different objectives, but at the end we are gaining the skills, that hopefully we can use futher in our lifes.

Different students were working on the different parts of the project in order to compile it into a single one. Jussi was making the engine for the game, Markus was responsible for the graphic design of it, Pia was creating the sounds, me - Evgenii, I have designed and coded this small website for our project.

The project as whole yet remains unfinished, however most parts of it are already done. If nothing goes wrong, we are hoping to release the game before the beginning of the final semester of 2018-2019 academic year.
